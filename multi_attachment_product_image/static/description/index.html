Upload Multiple Images at Once

Upload multiple images at once and save your time

By default, you can upload only a single image to product at once in Odoo. The module facilitates this process by uploading multiple images at a time.

How It Works

  • Open product form
  • Go to Images tab
  • Click Select Images

Select multiple images and click Open

As a result, all the selected images have been uploaded
